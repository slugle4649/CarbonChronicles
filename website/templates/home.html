Carbon Chronicles

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities to understand and address the impact of carbon emissions on our planet.

By raising awareness, inspiring action, and fostering collaboration, we aim to create a healthier, more resilient world for current and future generations.

Carbon Facts

Since 1970, CO2 emissions have increased by about 90%

The world is using the equivalent of 1.6 Earths of resources to maintain our current way of life, and ecosystems cannot keep up with our demands.

Adapting to climate consequences protects people, homes, businesses, livelihoods, infrastructure and natural ecosystems.
